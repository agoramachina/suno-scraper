Technical Details
Suno API Endpoints Used
1. Get all songs (paginated):

GET https://studio-api.prod.suno.com/api/feed/v2?hide_disliked=true&hide_gen_stems=true&hide_studio_clips=true&page=0
2. Get specific songs by IDs:

GET https://studio-api.prod.suno.com/api/clips/get_songs_by_ids?ids=xxx&ids=yyy
Authentication Required:

Header: authorization: Bearer <JWT_TOKEN>
Header: device-id: <UUID>
